Introduction

All our materials are free and open source.

You can freely print them out, use them at your home or other events.

Instructions

All materials come in two formats:

  • A6 - to read from screen. There is a single card per page in correct order to finish the booklet etc..
  • A4 - ready for pringing. Four A6 cards are put together per page to make a booklet. Sometimes several pages form a single booklet. After printing, you need to cut it and fold.
